Digital Payment Abhiyan
India has embarked on an ambitious transformation journey to realize its vision of a Digital India. Digital and Financial Inclusion are key enablers to march towards a Digitally empowered Society and a Knowledge Economy. With Government and Industry moving towards a 'Less Cash Economy', Digital Payment channels have caught momentum and citizens across the country are adopting various modes of digital payments. Government, Industry and all stakeholders in the digital payments ecosystem need to partner to build awareness among users about security and safely transact online.
